MonkeGamer Roku Privacy Policy

Welcome to MonkeGamer! We value your privacy and want to be transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Roku channel.

Data Collection

At MonkeGamer, we respect your privacy. We do not actively collect any personal information from you through our Roku channel. While data may be sent to our servers for technical reasons, we do not analyze or use this data unless necessary for troubleshooting or improving our services.

Information Usage

As we do not actively collect personal information, we do not use it for any purpose. Any data that may be transmitted to our servers is not used for marketing, profiling, or any other user-specific activities.

Data Retention

We retain any data sent to our servers only for as long as necessary to ensure the proper functioning of our Roku channel. We do not retain any data for user profiling or tracking purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties, except as required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at ubiquitousballs@gmail.com.
